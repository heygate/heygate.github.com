Heygate Was Home

This is a digital archive set up by the Southwark Notes Archive Group with the help of a grant from the Antipode Foundation. The interviews and research were carried out as part of a wider collaboration headed up by Professor Loretta Lees of King's College London and culminated in the publication of 'Staying Put' - an anti-gentrification handbook for council estates in London.
