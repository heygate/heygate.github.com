Government planning guidance defines Regeneration as the "environmental, economic and social improvement" of an area. This 'improvement' is usually measured using economic and deprivation indices, but this makes little sense when an entire community has been replaced by an other. Moreover, there appears to be very little research into measuring the real social impact of regeneration, so we decided to take part in a social research project by the Antipode foundation in order to gather a few testimonials testifying to our Elephant & Castle brand of regeneration. NB: the testimonials of Terry Redpath and Mojisola Ojeikery are verbatim reproductions of the witness evidence they gave to the 2013 Heygate CPO public inquiry. Those of Doreen Gee and Liz Grace are Transcript extracts from a short film documentary by Patrick Steel and David Reeve ('Doreen' (2012)).
